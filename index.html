Meat Calculator

This calculator helps you determine how much raw meat you need to cook to achieve your desired amount of cooked meat, taking into account shrinkage during cooking.

Instructions:

  1. Enter the amount of cooked meat you want per meal (in ounces).
  2. Enter the number of meals you want to prepare.
  3. Select the type of meat from the dropdown menu.
  4. If you select a ground meat, enter the fat percentage.
  5. Select your cooking method.
  6. Click "Calculate" to see the results.
